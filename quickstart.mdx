## Start Your Personal Growth Journey

Welcome to BetterSelf Daily! This guide will help you get up and running with your AI-powered journaling companion.

### Step 1: Create Your Account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Sign up for BetterSelf Daily">
    Visit [app.betterselfdaily.com](https://app.betterselfdaily.com) and create your account. You'll need:
    - A valid email address
    - A secure password
    - Your preferred display name

    <Tip>Use your real name to make your journal entries feel more personal!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="brain" title="Connect to Verbal AI">
    BetterSelf Daily is powered by Verbal's AI platform. During signup:
    1. You'll be prompted to authorize Verbal integration
    2. Choose your preferred AI model (GPT-4, Claude, or others)
    3. Set your privacy preferences

    <Note>Your journal entries are encrypted end-to-end for maximum privacy.</Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="pen" title="Set your personal goals">
    Tell BetterSelf Daily what you want to achieve:
    - Select areas of focus (health, career, relationships, mindfulness)
    - Set your journaling frequency preference
    - Choose notification preferences

    <Tip>The more specific your goals, the better AI can tailor insights for you!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="calendar" title="Schedule your daily check-in">
    Consistency is key to personal growth. Set up your daily routine:
    1. Choose your preferred journaling time
    2. Enable reminder notifications
    3. Set your timezone

    <Note>Most users find morning or evening reflections most effective.</Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Write Your First Entry

<Accordion icon="book-open" title="Start journaling">
  Ready to begin? Write your first journal entry:
  1. Click "New Entry" on your dashboard
  2. Answer the AI-generated prompts or free-write
  3. Submit your entry to receive instant AI insights

  <Tip>Your first entry helps calibrate the AI to your unique voice and style!</Tip>
</Accordion>
